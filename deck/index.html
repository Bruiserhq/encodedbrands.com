01 // The Problem

100+ Agents.
Zero Briefing.

Customer service bots. Search engine summaries. Commerce copilots. Internal tools. Social agents. Every one of them is representing your brand right now. None of them have been told what your brand is.

Perplexity answers about you. ChatGPT recommends your competitors. Shopify agents describe your products in someone else's voice. You have no input.
02 // The Gap

PDFs Are for Humans.
Machines Are Guessing.

Brand guidelines were designed for designers. They live in Dropbox. They're 80-page PDFs. No machine has ever parsed one. Your $200K brand system is invisible to the AI workforce that now represents you at scale.

Schema.org gives machines your address. It doesn't give them your soul.
03 // Brand.md

The System Prompt
for Your Organization.

Brand.md is a single, machine-readable specification that encodes your identity: voice, values, positioning, visual tokens, behavioral constraints. Not a document. Infrastructure.

One file. Every agent reads it. Every interaction reflects it. No manual prompting. No drift.
04 // Brand MCP

The Protocol That
Connects Everything.

Brand MCP is the real-time connectivity layer. Any AI agent in your stack, internal or external, connects to your Brand.md through the protocol. Persistent coherence. Representation control. Semantic governance.

Internal agents stay on-voice. External systems get structured identity data. One protocol governs both.
05 // The Architecture

Three Layers.
One Spec.

Internal Coherence: Every employee tool, every internal agent, pulls brand context automatically. No more "write in our voice" prompts.

External Representation: Search engines, recommendation systems, and commerce agents get structured data about who you are.

Semantic Governance: One file governs behavior, not just pixels. "Does this agent's output reflect our positioning?"
