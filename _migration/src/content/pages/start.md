callout_2_label: Which AI to use
callout_2: Works well with <b>Claude</b> (any model), <b>ChatGPT</b> (GPT-4+), <b>Cowork</b>, and <b>Gemini 3.1 Pro</b>. Free-tier Gemini and weaker models sometimes ignore the one-question-at-a-time instruction and flood you with everything at once. If that happens, tell the AI "<i>stop — ask one question, wait for my reply</i>." If it still misbehaves, switch to a smarter model.
step2_idx: Step 02
step2_sub: 15 minutes
step2_heading: Answer the <em>interview</em>.
step2_intro: The AI will ask you eighteen questions across six parts. Answer them in your own words. <b>Be specific.</b> Vague inputs produce vague BCPs — and a vague BCP does nothing for your brand.
step2_list_intro: "You'll cover:"
step2_items:
  - text: <b>Identity</b> — who you are, who you serve, how you're different from your competitors.
  - text: <b>Voice</b> — how you sound, how you don't, what to prefer, what to avoid.
  - text: <b>Values</b> — three to five values in priority order, each with an observable behavior that shows you mean it. Talk is cheap.
  - text: <b>Boundaries</b> — the hard no's and the soft no's. Brand safety signals your vendor platforms can read.
  - text: <b>Claims</b> — factual statements you make about yourself, with the evidence behind each one.
  - text: <b>Representation</b> — the exact language you want ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini to use when they describe you.
step2_outro: At the end, the AI gives you seven markdown files — a root <code>brand.md</code> plus six daughter files. That's your complete BCP tree.
