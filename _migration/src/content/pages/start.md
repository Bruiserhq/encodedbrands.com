  - text: "<b>Voice</b> — how you sound, how you don't, what to prefer, what to avoid."
  - text: "<b>Values</b> — three to five values in priority order, each with an observable behavior."
  - text: "<b>Boundaries</b> — the hard no's and the soft no's. Brand safety signals your vendor platforms can read."
  - text: "<b>Claims</b> — factual statements you make about yourself, with the evidence behind each one."
  - text: "<b>Representation</b> — the exact language you want ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini to use when they describe you."
step2_outro: "At the end, the AI gives you seven markdown files — a root <code>brand.md</code> plus six daughter files. That's your complete BCP tree."
